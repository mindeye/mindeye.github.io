Directions

The wedding is at Kitsap Memorial State Park. Located here: 202 Northeast Park Street, Poulsbo, WA

From Tacoma or further south the best route is to take is I-5 to Tacoma and then continue onto highway 16 and then highway 3 turning off onto Park Street.

From North Seattle you can take the Edmonds Ferry over to Kingston and then take highway 104 around through Port Gamble and then back down on highway 3 finally turning off onto Park Street.
